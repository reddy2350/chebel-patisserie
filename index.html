— Our Promise —

Seven years. Finest ingredients. Masterful craft.

Chebel began as a quiet pursuit of the perfect bite — a way of returning to the flavours of childhood through the discipline of European patisserie. Today, each cake is still composed by hand, in small batches, by chefs who believe a dessert should be felt as much as it is tasted.

— Our Philosophy —

A dessert is a small piece of memory.

"Our desserts evoke cherished taste memory — the first cake at a grandmother's table, a midnight slice shared in confidence."

Every recipe at Chebel begins with a question: what does this remind us of? From there we work backwards — sourcing single-origin couverture, hand-churning our butter creams, weighing flours to the gram. Our masterful chefs treat each celebration as if it were their own.

Yara Chebel
Founder & Chef Pâtissier
