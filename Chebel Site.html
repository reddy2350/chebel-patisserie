— The Process —

From the carte to the table, in three quiet steps.

i.

Reserve

Choose from the carte, or compose a signature with the kitchen. Reservations open seventy-two hours ahead — same-day for select pastries.

ii.

Composed by Hand

The kitchen weighs, layers, and glazes each cake the morning of pick-up. Nothing is held overnight; nothing is shipped frozen.

iii.

Collected, or Delivered

Walk into the Atelier on Rue des Cèdres, or have it delivered citywide — wrapped in our signature ribbon, kept at temperature.

— Our Story —

Born in Beirut,
inspired by memory.

Founded in 2019 by Yara Chebel, the Maison is a quiet pursuit of the perfect bite — a way of returning to the flavours of childhood through the discipline of European patisserie.

Every recipe begins with a question: what does this remind us of? We work backwards from the flavour of memory until the cake feels familiar.

— Yara Chebel
Chef & Founder
